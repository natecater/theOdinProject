Quick Chicken Pho

Quick weeknight chicken pho from expert Andrea Nguyen! Shortcut version of traditional Vietnamese noodle soup. 30-minutes.

  1. Prepare the broth ingredients
  2. Toast the broth ingredients
  3. Add the broth and bring to a simmer
  4. Remove the chicken from the broth once cooked
  5. Simmer the broth
  6. Shred the chicken
  7. Soak the rice noodles
  8. Strain the broth
  9. Finish the pho
