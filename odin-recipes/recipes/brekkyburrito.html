Breakfast Burrito Recipe

My own personal favorite breakfast burrito recipe (or one of them) off the top of my head.

  1. Start cooking diced potatoes with butter
  2. Add in chopped sausage or bacon after potatoes are slightly golden/brown
  3. Let potatoes and sausage cook for a while
  4. (Optional) Chop up some sweet peppers and add for more flavor
  5. Add spinach, let it cook down, and add more spinach
  6. Use however many eggs you want (3-4 for me) and cook in with the rest
  7. After it's finished cooking, set to the side and crisp/prepare your tortilla in a skillet
  8. Add eggs, potatotes, sausages, and spinach to the tortilla
  9. Take some avocado or guac and slap that on top
  10. Wrap your burrito, and have it with some Cholula and/or your favorite salsa
